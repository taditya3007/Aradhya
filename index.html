Who We Are

Aradhya Ventures is an integrated construction, infrastructure development and management company which has been built on the basis of Ganesh Group of companies. Ganesh Group of companies has expanded its wings in various sectors.

GANESH Group consists of the following firms catering to different sectors of the industry.
  • Ganesh Rolling Shutters And Engineering works Estd 1968
  • Ganesh Sand Blasting Works Estd. 1994
  • Sri Sai Balaji Engineering Estd. 2000
  • M.Ranga Reddy & Sons. Estd. 1992
  • Aradhya Ventures. Estd. 2016
  • Aradhya Constructions DMCC (Dubai). Estd. 2017

Vision

We are a family owned company, committed to becoming the contractor of choice, pursuing excellence through dedication, experience and disciplined employees with an ongoing passion to deliver quality, timely and profitable projects.

Mission

We will deliver reliable, high-quality solutions for global infrastructure, always ensuring that integrity, safety and sustainability are at the heart of everything we do and also to provide construction and asset management solutions in an environment where people feel safe, secure and valued.

Values

We believe in having Dedication, Commitment, Ethics, Reliability, Safety, Quality, Trust, Integrity, Measured Performance, Expertise. Ganesh Rolling Shutters and Engineering works is engaged in manufacturing Rolling Shutters, Steel Windows, Steel Doors, Pre Engineered Buildings, Tubular and Angular Structures, and also Government Building Contractors.

Ganesh Sand Blasting Works is engaged in Sand, Shot, Grit, Copper Slag Blasting, and Metallizing (Aluminum and Zinc Metal Spray), All Kinds of Industrial Painting.

Sri Sai Balaji Engineering is engaged in manufacture of Aluminium Corona Shields, Aluminium Corona Rings and Switchgear related components. M.Ranga Reddy & Sons is into Film Exhibition consisting of Group of Theatres Ganesh 70mm, Ranga 70mm, Sai Ranga 70mm.

Aradhya Ventures mainly focuses on manufacturing activity of steel buildings (pre- engineered buildings), in the oil and natural gas, industrial construction sector and also specialize in surface finishing and industrial coatings (TSA coatings, Anti slip coatings, hot-dip galvanizing, metal spray, epoxy coatings, polyurethane coatings, powder coating), pipeline coatings (cement guniting, food grade epoxy coatings).

In Oil and Natural gas sector we have acquired international projects which is to be operated by our subsidiary Aradhya Constructions DMCC, Dubai, UAE.
